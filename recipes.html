Homemade Pizza

Origin: Friend's Recipe

Category: Main Dish

homemade_pizza.jpg

My girlfriend's mom made pizzas a long time ago and we wanted to make one from scratch. We asked her for a generic set of instructions for a basic but still good pizza. We added whatever any of us wanted as toppings and you can too!

>Recipe Ingredients

  1. Warm Water
  2. Rapid Rise Yeast
  3. Bread Flour
  4. Salt
  5. Cornmeal
  6. Olive Oil
  7. Canned Peeled Tomatoes
  8. Garlic
  9. Ground Black Pepper
  10. Granulated Sugar
  11. Dried Oregano
  12. Parmesan Cheese
  13. Whole Milk Mozzarella
  14. Pepperonis and/or other toppings

Recipe Steps

  1. Mix 2 cups water, 2 tsp yeast, and 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Seperately, preferably in a mixer, 4 cups mix bread flour and 2 tsp salt
  3. Pour yeast mixture into stand mixer with the flour, knead on medium speed (dough hook works well)
  4. Take out dough ball, place in a lightly oiled bowl and let rise 2 hours
  5. "Punch" down the dough and split it in 2
  6. Around now would be a good time to heat up your oven (500 degrees) for a bit if you plan to use a pizza stone (heat at least 30 minutes)
  7. Place parchment paper on your pizza peel (or whatever you can use as a substitute) and lighty cover in cornmeal
  8. Stretch out dough, by hanf in the air, then place on top of cornmeal and continue stretching until satisfied
  9. Brush olive oil on outside edge of dough
  10. "Drain" your tomatoes
  11. Preferably in as mixer, mix tomatoes with 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp granulated sugar, 0.25 tsp ground black pepper, 1 tsp salt, and 1 minced garlic clove
  12. Pour some sauce on pizza and spread it around
  13. Sprinkle about a half cup of parmesan around on pizza
  14. Put 2 cups mozzarella
  15. Cover with desired toppings and desired amount of each
  16. Cook around 12-15 minutes

Butter Chicken

Origin: Changed a Friend's Recipe

Category: Main Dish

butter_chicken.jpg

My girlfriend's dad made butter chicken once and we've been making it from time to time ever since. We have our own set of ingridients/small changes made but its still pretty similar and very good.

>Recipe Ingredients

  1. Chicken Breast (1-4 pounds)
  2. Butter (1/4 cup per pound of chicken)
  3. Onions (1 per pound of chicken)
  4. Bell Pepper (1 per pound of chicken)
  5. Garlic (2 cloves per pound of chicken)
  6. Green onion (1 per pound of chicken)
  7. Diced Tomato Cans (1 per 2 pounds of chicken)
  8. Ginger Powder (1 tsp per pound of chicken)
  9. Curry Powder (2 tbsp per pound of chicken)
  10. Cumin (1 tsp per pound of chicken)
  11. Turmeric (1 tsp per pound of chicken)
  12. Paprika (1 tsp per pound of chicken)
  13. Salt (1 tsp per pound of chicken)
  14. Ground Pepper (1 tsp per pound of chicken)
  15. Optional: Make rice as well

Recipe Steps

  1. Clean and dice chicken
  2. Wash and dice onions, peppers, and green onions
  3. Mince garlic
  4. Prepare all spices by putting correct amounts in a small bowl
  5. In a big enough pot, melt the butter and then partially fry all of the chicken in it (no browning/crisping)
  6. Then add in seasonings and mix thoroughly
  7. Next add in all remaining ingredients and mix again
  8. Cover and let cook 20 minutes in medium heat
  9. Remove lid and cook another 20 minutes
  10. Optional: About 5-10 minutes before it is done, you can add plain yougurt or a similar substitute and mix in (around 1 "regular" tsp scoop per pound of chicken)
  11. Mix a serving in a bowl with rice if desired/made some

Roast

Origin: Family Recipe

Category: Main Dish

roast.jpg

My dad's recipe for his take on a roast that I've been having for most of my life. Very delicious and easy to make.

>Recipe Ingredients

  1. 3-4 Pounds Chicken Drumsticks
  2. 6-8 Potatoes
  3. 3-4 Onions
  4. 9-12 Carrots
  5. Oil
  6. Salt
  7. Pepper
  8. Any other spices you would deem fit for a simple dish like this

Recipe Steps

  1. Oil big baking pan/pot
  2. Put chicken in pot and leave it on the stove top on medium heat till right before they start browing
  3. Put rest of ingreidients in pot and put pot in oven for 30-40 minutes at 375 degrees
  4. Test potatoes and check a piece of chicken to make sure it is all cooked enough
